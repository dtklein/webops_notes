Agile is a set of project management processes that together help accelerate results and shorten time to market, by iteratively releasing small, atomic deliverables in a short amount of time. 

 - Do not let perfection prevent releasing something good
 - It is preferable to reduce scope and release now, rather than delay a release
 - If releases hurt and are scary, release often; you will master it or automate it, and it won't be scary
